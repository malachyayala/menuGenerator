Okay, here is a 7-day meal plan based on your inventory, preferences, and dietary goals. I've tried to incorporate variety, minimize waste, and make the most of what you already have.

**Monday**

*   **Day of the Week:** Monday
*   **Recipe Name:** Spicy Pork Gyoza Noodle Soup
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Pork Gyoza Potstickers: Around 12 (half the pack)
    *   Plain Ramen Noodles: 2 Packs (Koyo brand)
    *   Chili Powder: 1 tsp
    *   Chopped Garlic: 1 tbsp
    *   Soy Sauce: Needed (Shopping List - Ramen Soup), can sub with salt if desired
    *   Sesame Oil: Needed (Shopping List - Ramen Soup)
*   **Detailed Instructions:**
    1.  Cook Ramen Noodles according to package directions. Drain (reserve some cooking water if desired).
    2.  In the same pot, heat a little Sesame Oil (add to shopping list) over medium heat.
    3.  Add Chopped Garlic and Chili Powder, cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
    4.  Add Soy Sauce, a cup of water, and bring to a simmer.
    5.  Add cooked Ramen Noodles and Pork Gyoza Potstickers. Cook until gyoza are heated through.
    6.  Serve hot.
*   **Estimated Prep Time:** 5 minutes
*   **Estimated Cook Time:** 15 minutes
*   **Original Recipe Link:** Modified from various ramen recipes.
*   **Leftover Potential:** Leftovers are not recommended due to the noodles getting soggy.

**Tuesday**

*   **Day of the Week:** Tuesday
*   **Recipe Name:** Chicken Thigh Tacos with Chipotle-Lime Crema
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Chicken Thighs: 0.5 lb (approx. 2 thighs)
    *   Corn Tortillas: 4 tortillas
    *   Chipotle Chili Powder: 0.5 tsp
    *   Lime Juice: 1 tbsp
    *   Mayo: 1 tbsp
    *   Salt: 1/4 tsp
    *   Black Pepper: Needed (Shopping List - Chicken Tacos)
    *   Avocado: Needed (Shopping List - Chicken Tacos)
*   **Detailed Instructions:**
    1.  Season chicken thighs with salt, black pepper (add to shopping list), and Chipotle Chili Powder.
    2.  Cook chicken thighs in a pan over medium heat until cooked through (about 8-10 minutes per side). Shred or dice.
    3.  Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
    4.  Mix Mayo with Lime Juice for the crema.
    5.  Assemble tacos with chicken, chipotle-lime crema, and diced Avocado (add to shopping list).
*   **Estimated Prep Time:** 10 minutes
*   **Estimated Cook Time:** 20 minutes
*   **Original Recipe Link:** Inspired by various taco recipes.
*   **Leftover Potential:** Leftover chicken is good for salads or sandwiches the next day.

**Wednesday**

*   **Day of the Week:** Wednesday
*   **Recipe Name:** Grassfed Ground Bison Chili
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Grassfed Ground Bison: 0.5 lb
    *   Pink Beans: 1 cup
    *   Diced Green Chiles: 0.5 cup
    *   Chili Powder: 1 tbsp
    *   Cumin: 1 tsp
    *   Cayenne Pepper: 1/4 tsp (adjust to taste)
    *   Tomato Paste: 1 tbsp
    *   Beef Stock: 6 oz
    *   Onion: Needed (Shopping List - Bison Chili) - Use garlic powder as a sub for onion taste
    *   Bell Pepper: Needed (Shopping List - Bison Chili)
*   **Detailed Instructions:**
    1.  Cook the ground bison in a pot with chili powder, cumin, and cayenne pepper until browned. Drain excess fat.
    2.  Add the garlic powder (subbing for onion), tomato paste, pink beans, diced green chilies, and beef stock.
    3.  Simmer for at least 30 minutes, or longer for a thicker chili.
*   **Estimated Prep Time:** 15 minutes
*   **Estimated Cook Time:** 45 minutes
*   **Original Recipe Link:** Inspired by various chili recipes.
*   **Leftover Potential:** Excellent for leftovers! Chili freezes well. Serve with cheese and sour cream (not in inventory).

**Thursday**

*   **Day of the Week:** Thursday
*   **Recipe Name:** Spicy Gochujang Chicken Thighs with Rice Paper Wraps
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Chicken Thighs: 0.5 lb (approx. 2 thighs)
    *   Gochujang: 2 tbsp
    *   Soy Sauce: Needed (Shopping List - Rice Paper Wraps)
    *   Sesame Oil: Needed (Shopping List - Rice Paper Wraps)
    *   Garlic: Needed (Shopping List - Rice Paper Wraps)
    *   Ginger: Needed (Shopping List - Rice Paper Wraps)
    *   Unpolished Rice Paper: 6 Sheets
    *   Peanut Butter: 0.5 tbsp
    *   Cucumber: Needed (Shopping List - Rice Paper Wraps)
    *   Carrots: Needed (Shopping List - Rice Paper Wraps)
*   **Detailed Instructions:**
    1.  Marinate chicken thighs in a mixture of Gochujang, Sesame Oil, Garlic, Ginger, and Soy Sauce (add to shopping list) for at least 30 minutes.
    2.  Grill, bake, or pan-fry chicken until cooked through. Shred or dice.
    3.  Prepare rice paper according to package instructions.
    4.  Assemble rice paper wraps with chicken, cucumber, and carrots. Dip in peanut butter.
*   **Estimated Prep Time:** 20 minutes (including marinating time)
*   **Estimated Cook Time:** 20 minutes
*   **Original Recipe Link:** Inspired by Korean BBQ and rice paper wrap recipes.
*   **Leftover Potential:** Leftover chicken is good for stir-fries or salads.

**Saturday**

*   **Day of the Week:** Saturday
*   **Recipe Name:** Ground Pork Stir-Fry with Rotini
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Ground Pork: 0.5 lb
    *   Rotini: 8 oz
    *   Chopped Garlic: 1 tsp
    *   Sweet Chili Sauce: 2 tbsp
    *   Sesame Oil: Needed (Shopping List - Stir-fry)
    *   Soy Sauce: Needed (Shopping List - Stir-fry)
    *   Ginger: Needed (Shopping List - Stir-fry)
    *   Vegetables (Broccoli, Carrots, Peppers): Needed (Shopping List - Stir-fry)
*   **Detailed Instructions:**
    1. Cook Rotini according to package instructions.
    2. Cook the pork in a pan with soy sauce, chopped garlic, sesame oil, and chopped ginger. 
    3. Add vegetables and sweet chili sauce and cook until tender.
    4. Serve the rotini with the pork and vegetable stir-fry.
*   **Estimated Prep Time:** 15 minutes
*   **Estimated Cook Time:** 30 minutes
*   **Original Recipe Link:** Inspired by various stir-fry recipes.
*   **Leftover Potential:** Leftovers will stay in the fridge for 2 days.

**Sunday**

*   **Day of the Week:** Sunday
*   **Recipe Name:** Chicken Bouillon Soup with Corn Tortilla Strips
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Chicken Bouillon: 2 Packets
    *   Corn Tortillas: 2 tortillas
    *   Garlic Powder: 0.5 tsp
    *   Cumin: 0.25 tsp
    *   Limes: Needed (Shopping List - Chicken Soup)
    *   Cilantro: Needed (Shopping List - Chicken Soup)
*   **Detailed Instructions:**
    1.  Boil 4 cups of water. Add Chicken Bouillon, garlic powder, and cumin. Simmer for 15 minutes.
    2. Cut corn tortillas into strips and bake them until crunchy.
    3. Season the tortilla strips with salt.
    4. Serve soup topped with the tortilla strips. Season with cilantro and lime.
*   **Estimated Prep Time:** 15 minutes
*   **Estimated Cook Time:** 30 minutes
*   **Original Recipe Link:** Modified chicken soup
*   **Leftover Potential:** Leftover chicken broth is good for lunches.
